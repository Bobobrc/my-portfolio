About DailySnap

The app combines social media with a competitive twist. Users can post one photo each day and compete on daily leaderboards based on the likes they receive. Each user can like only one post per day, which makes every like meaningful and encourages engagement.

The app also features streaks and achievements that reward users for posting consistently or completing specific goals. Communities play a major role as well; users can create public or private groups, each with its own themed leaderboard, allowing people to compete in smaller, more personalized spaces.

Log in & Sign up

Users sign up with a username, email, and password, and then log in using their email and password. Since this is a demo version, the email does not need to be valid.

Main & Profile

After logging in, you’re taken to the main screen, where you can view all posts from that day, sorted by the number of likes. You can like a post by double-tapping it, and you can also log out from this screen.

There is also a profile section where you can see all the posts in your current streak for each community you’re part of. You start and maintain a streak by posting one photo per day in that community.

Camera

The camera screen is simple and intuitive, featuring controls for flash, switching cameras, and adding a timer.

After taking a photo, you’re taken to a preview screen where you can select the communities you want to post it in. From there, you can retake the photo or confirm and publish it.

Communities

Communities are a core part of the app, each with its own daily leaderboard. Users can create a community with just a name, and optionally add a description or a community picture.
